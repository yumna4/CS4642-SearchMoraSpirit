Talent cannot be limited. Talent goes forward breaking every limit. Even the maxima can be exceeded by the talent. Sports is the arena for the best examples.

SLUG or Sri Lanka University Games is a best match for exposing better and improved talent of the university sportsmen and sportswomen. It is an ideal opportunity to break records and place new ones.

Today, on the second day of SLUG 2016, sports fans who gathered at Kuliyapitiya University Premises were felicitous to witness a breaking of record in Weightlifting.

Under the weight class of 77kg, with the total weight of 225kg, representing University of Ruhuna, S. T. Warnasinghe was able to break the SLUG weightlifting record which was owned by M. D. S. Duminda,with the total weight of 115kg, representing University of Moratuwa at SLUG-2010.

We, Morasirit would like to convey our heartiest congratulations to the new record owner and wish good luck to all the participants of SLUG 2016 for strength and courageous to place more and more records and uplift the value of university sports in Sri Lanka
